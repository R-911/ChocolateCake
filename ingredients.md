The ingredients you need are as follows:

1.5 cup whole wheat flour,
2-3 tablespoons of cocoa powder,
1 cup curd,
Half cup vegetable oil,
1 cup sugar,
A pinch of salt,
1 teaspoon choclate essence,
1 teaspoon baking powder,
1 teaspoon baking soda.

Optional:

Chocolate Chips,
Walnuts,
Almonds.
